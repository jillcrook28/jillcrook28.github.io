Things to do

Here are some of the things we can do at the locations we're visiting!

Temple

Experience the beauty of Bangkok's temples during our family trip. Visit Wat Arun, a captivating temple on the riverbank, with its stunning spires and picturesque views.

The Grand Palace

Located in Bangkok, the Grand Palace is a beautiful royal villa that was home to Thai kings until 1932. There is a dress code- men must wear long pants, women must cover legs to above the knee, and no tight or shoulder-revealing clothing. There is also an entrance fee of 500 baht.

Chai Lai Orchid

Embark on a captivating journey to Thailand's breathtaking Chai Lai Orchid, where lush jungles, majestic elephants, and authentic Thai hospitality await, offering our family an enchanting escape filled with cherished moments and unforgettable adventures in nature's embrace. Immerse yourselves in a harmonious blend of beauty and cultural immersion, creating memories that will forever linger in our hearts.
